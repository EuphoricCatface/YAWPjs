Yet Another Word Puzzle

Thread the letters into a word for scores. Get highest score in 20 turns!

Attention! This game is not completed. TODO is written at the bottom of the page. Stay tuned!

How to play: Drag your mouse cursor to thread the tiles. When the threaded tiles make a word, they get squashed!

Created by EuphCat. Based on Wordle by FatCat Studios Ltd (No, not *the* Wordle by Josh Wardle).

Initial styling was done by referring to gabrielecirulli/2048, because this is my first HTML5 project.
